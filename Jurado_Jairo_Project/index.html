CONMEBOL WORLD CUP STATS

The South American Football Confederation or as it is known in Spanish: Confederación Sudamericana de Fútbol, or CONMEBOL for short, is the official South American Continental governing body for FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association). The CONMEBOL is made of 10 team members: Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Paraguay, Perú, Uruguay, and Venezuela.

The CONMEBOL 2014 Brazil World Cup Qualifiers have ended as of December 19th, 2013. The teams that qualified were: Argentina, Colombia, Chile, Ecuador, Uruguay, and Brazil. Brazil qualified by default because the 2014 World Cup will be held in Brazil.
